  • What is RateMy?
  • RateMy is a rating and discussion website. Registered members can create categories in which other registered members can upload images and text posts. These posts can be upvoted/downvoted by fellow members.
  • How can I create a Category?
  • You have to be logged in first and click on the dropdown menu with your username and you should see a link to the "Create a Category" page
  • How can I create a Post?
  • First be a logged in. Then navigate to the category you want to post to and there you will find an "Add Post" button
  • How can I comment?
  • To be able to comment you must have a logged in Facebook account.
